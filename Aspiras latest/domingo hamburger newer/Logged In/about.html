Our Sustainable Story

Redefining fashion with purpose and style

Our Story

Urban Thrift began with a simple mission: to make sustainable fashion accessible and stylish.

We believe in giving clothing a second life, reducing waste, and curating timeless pieces that fit modern lifestyles. Every item we offer is carefully selected for quality, style, and sustainability â€” because we believe fashion should be both beautiful and responsible.

Founded in 2018 by fashion enthusiast and environmental advocate Mia Rodriguez, Urban Thrift started as a small pop-up shop in Manila. What began as a passion project quickly grew into a movement as more people embraced sustainable fashion.

Today, we partner with local communities to source high-quality pre-loved items while supporting fair trade practices. Our team of stylists ensures each piece meets our standards before it reaches you.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the fashion industry by making sustainable shopping the first choice for conscious consumers, proving that style and environmental responsibility can go hand in hand.

Sustainability

We champion eco-friendly practices and reduce textile waste by promoting second-hand fashion. Each purchase saves approximately 1,800 gallons of water.

Quality

Every piece is inspected to ensure it meets our standards of durability and timeless style. We accept only items in excellent condition or better.

Community

We support local sellers and foster a community of conscious shoppers. 30% of our profits go back to environmental initiatives in our communities.
